Meet Our Partners

Rexall: is a approached household brand and together with Sobeys Pharmacy represents the ValueHealth™ retail pharmacy network. Rexall Direct is an integral part of the Rexall family, and provides the ValueHealthM pharmacy mail order delivery service.

McKesson, ValueHealth's™ managing partner, provides McKesson Specialty pharmacy navigation and case management services for patient support and assistance as well as dispensing of specialty DINS that cannot be dispensed by Sobeys and Rexall stores.

Sobeys retail and grocery brands include Lawtons Drugs, Sobeys Pharmacy, Pharmacy at Safeway, Freshco Pharmacy, Foodland Pharmacy, Thrifty Foods Pharmacy and IGA Pharmacy.

ClaimSecure provides management and adjudication services for all drug, dental and extended healthcare claims with authorization for specialty drugs.
